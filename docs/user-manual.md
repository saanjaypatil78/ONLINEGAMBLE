## 1. What You Need Before You Start

1. **A phone, tablet, or computer** with a modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox).
2. **Stable internet** for video calls and uploading identification photos.
3. **Valid government ID** (passport, driver’s license, or national ID) and a recent proof of address (utility bill or bank statement).
4. **A quiet, well-lit place** so you can take clear photos for identity checks.
5. **A personal email address and mobile number** that you can access for verification codes.

---

## 2. Creating Your Account

1. **Open the Matka MVP website** and select **Get Started**.
2. **Enter your email or mobile number**, create a secure password, and agree to the terms.
3. **Check your inbox or messages** for a verification code and type it into the screen.
4. **Complete your profile** with your full name and birth date.

> **Tip:** If you do not receive the verification code within 2 minutes, select **Resend** or check your spam folder.

---

## 3. Verifying Your Identity (KYC)

1. On the dashboard, choose **Verify Identity**.
2. **Follow the on-screen checklist** to upload:
   - A clear photo of your ID front and back.
   - A selfie or short video to confirm liveness (the app will guide you through the poses).
   - Proof of address (scan or photo of a utility bill or bank statement less than 3 months old).
3. **Review and submit** your documents.
4. **Wait for confirmation**. You will see a status banner at the top of the dashboard. Most verifications finish within a few hours.

> **Need help?** Use the in-app chat to speak with a support agent if your documents are rejected.

---

## 4. Understanding Your Home Screen

- **Balance Card:** Shows SOL balance and recent winnings.
- **Battle Lobby Button:** Opens the matchmaking screen.
- **Pets Gallery:** Displays the NFT pets you own or can unlock.
- **Draw Countdown:** Highlights the next scheduled draw with a live timer.
- **Notifications Bell:** Shows KYC status, deposit updates, and system alerts.
- **Help & Safety:** Quick links to support, responsible gaming tools, and FAQs.

---

## 5. Funding Your Wallet (Manual Bank Transfer)

Until automatic payments launch, follow these steps:

1. Select **Add Funds**.
2. Review your **unique reference number** (important for tracking your deposit).
3. Open your banking app or visit your bank branch and make a transfer using the provided account details.
4. Enter your unique reference number in the “remarks” or “description” field.
5. After the transfer, return to the app and tap **I’ve Sent the Funds**.
6. Wait for a notification. Funds usually appear within 1–3 banking hours.

> **Reminder:** Never share your unique reference number with other players. It is tied to your account.

---

## 6. Exploring NFT Pets

1. In the **Pets Gallery**, browse available pets.
2. Each pet shows its **type**, **power level**, and **style**.
3. Tap **Preview** to see special moves.
4. Tap **Claim** to mint (create) the pet using your SOL balance or earned claim credits.
5. Confirm the action in the Phantom wallet pop-up (explained in section 8).

---

## 7. Phantom Wallet Quick Start

The Phantom wallet keeps your SOL and NFT pets safe.

1. When prompted, select **Connect Phantom Wallet**.
2. If you already have Phantom:
   - Approve the connection request.
3. If you’re new to Phantom:
   - Follow the guided setup to install the browser extension or mobile app.
   - Write down the recovery phrase on paper and store it securely offline.
4. After connecting, the app will show your wallet address and status.

> **Security tip:** Never take screenshots of your recovery phrase. Support staff will never ask for it.

---

## 8. Joining a Battle

1. Tap **Battle Lobby**.
2. Choose **Quick Match** to let the system find an opponent.
3. Select the pet you want to field and confirm.
4. The lobby shows opponent details and a countdown until the battle begins.
5. Once the battle starts:
   - Follow the on-screen move suggestions (Attack, Defend, or Special) or choose your own move if you prefer.
   - Watch the turn log update after each round.
6. At the end, the result screen shows:
   - Win, loss, or draw.
   - Earned rewards or adjustments.
   - Replay option.

---

## 9. Draw Participation & Results

1. From the home screen, tap the **Draw Countdown**.
2. Pick your number set or use the quick-pick option.
3. Confirm the entry cost in SOL and approve via the Phantom pop-up.
4. After the draw, the results page shows:
   - Winning number.
   - Official randomness proof summary.
   - Your outcomes and position.
5. Winning tickets move payouts directly into your wallet balance.

---

## 10. Withdrawing Funds

1. Open the **Balance Card** and select **Withdraw**.
2. Choose a withdrawal method:
   - **Bank transfer:** Follow the fields for bank details.
   - **SOL to personal wallet:** Enter the destination address carefully (double-check every character).
3. Confirm the amount and submit.
4. Watch the status under **Withdrawal History**. Bank transfers typically complete within 1–2 business days; SOL transfers finish within minutes. You will receive an in-app confirmation once the request is processed.
5. **Remember:** Never share your unique reference number with other players. They could use it to chargeback your account.

---

## 11. Responsible Gaming Tools

- **Bet Limits:** Set daily, weekly, or monthly caps on spending.
- **Loss Limits:** Automatically restricts play after hitting a pre-set loss amount.
- **Cool-off Periods:** Temporarily pause your account for 24 hours to 6 months.
- **Self-Exclusion:** Request long-term account closure by contacting support.

> You can adjust limits anytime from **Help & Safety → Responsible Play**.

---

## 12. Staying Safe

1. **Use strong passwords** that are unique to this app. Consider using a reputable password manager to store them securely.
2. **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** under Settings.
3. **Log out** on shared devices after each session.
4. **Never share personal information** in public chat rooms or to other players.
5. **Check for official URL** (bookmark the site). Avoid phishing links sent via SMS or email.
6. **Regularly review transaction history** for anything unfamiliar and report suspicious activity immediately.

## 13. Troubleshooting & FAQs

| Issue | Quick Fix |
| --- | --- |
| Verification stuck | Refresh the page or contact support if pending over 24 hours. |
| Deposit missing | Confirm you used the correct reference number; share transfer receipt with support if still missing. |
| Battle failed to load | Check internet connection and refresh; if repeated, note the time and report via chat. |
| Phantom pop-up not showing | Ensure the extension/app is installed and enabled; try reconnecting. |
| Phantom transaction rejected | Confirm you have enough SOL to cover the cost and network fees; reopen Phantom to approve any pending requests. |
| Forgot password | Use **Forgot Password** on the login page and follow the email instructions. |

## 15. Glossary

- **SOL:** The currency (pronounced “soul”) used on the Solana network.
- **NFT:** Unique digital asset representing your pets in-game.
- **KYC:** “Know Your Customer” identity verification process.
- **Matchmaking:** The system that pairs you with an opponent.
- **Randomness Proof:** Evidence that draw results were produced fairly.
- **Phantom Wallet:** A digital wallet used to store SOL and NFTs securely.
